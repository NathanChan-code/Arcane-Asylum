about the game

Game master, It is your turn to roll

Welcome to your Dungeon campain, take on the forest, to the wastelands and mountains with stategy in this tower defence game, Test your might against the goblin and ghouls which roam the forest, the trolls which reside in the mountains and the necromancer who has put despair in the kingdom of Lunaris

features

towers

3 basic towers which have their own unique properties and advantages against the enemies, with upgradedable paths and special abilites to customize how you play

Enemies

Face off against the the wild horde of enemies who have their own skills, strengths and weaknesses that will have you making constant tactical choices to fend them off

!-- Feature 3 -->

Dice Roll

Roll a dice and test your luck, the dice will reward your bravery or punish it, how you deal with the outcome is in your hands
